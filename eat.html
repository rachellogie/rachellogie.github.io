Fave recipe this week:

almond flour vegan biscuits

You will need:
  • 2 1/2 cups blanched almond flour
  • 2 tablespoons of flaxseeds, ground
  • 5 tablespoons of water
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
Instructions:
  1. Combine all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. After grinding up the flaxseeds, mix with the 5 tablespoons of water.
  3. Combine all wet ingredients in a bowl.
  4. Mix the dry ingredients into the wet.
  5. Form into biscuits.
  6. Bake at 350 for 15 minutes.
